Rock, Paper, Scissors Against AI

In this project you play rock, paper scissors against a bot, and everytime you lose you get insulted by Chat GPT.

  • View Project

On Start

On start you are asked to enter a choice of "r" for Rock, "p" for Paper, "s" for Scissor.

Player Wins Scenario

After player has chosen their option, they are shown the option they chose. Under that they are shown the score of the player and of the computer, under that the option the user and the computer both chose and then an option to play again.

    Computer wins Scenario

    After player has chosen their option, they are the option they chose. Under that they are shown who won. Under that the score. Under that its the option the player and the computer chose. Under that its an unique insult created by chat GBT. In this instance it says "You must be the most incompetent rock paper scissors player in the history of mankind. It's like watching a chimp try to figure out quantum physics. Stick to something easier, like breathing." To motivate the user into becoming a better player. Under that is an option to play again.

    Creating Insult

    Sending a prompt to Chat GBT and returning the response to the user.

    Main Logic

    Creating the scoring for both player and computer. Creating an array of action the player and computer choose from. A random choice is assigned to the computer. Both choices are sent to 'winner' method to determine who won and the answer is stored inside the result variable.

    Determine Winner Logic

    Retreiving the sent variables. Comparing the both the inputs to determine who is the winner and returning the winner back to the Main.
